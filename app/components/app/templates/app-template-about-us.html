About Us

Free, Accessible and Opensource Ride Sharing - this is the goal of ridesharemarket.com.

You are welcome to view all the code, though sensitive data like passwords and API keys are encrypted for security.

The code base is under active development and is available at github.com/ride-share-market

If you have any questions or wish to contribute please contact us.

How Ride Share Market Works

ridesharemarket.com exists simply to help each other share our travel resources.

We also feel it's best to try not travel with a stranger so we incorporate social networking with Facebook, Linkedin and Google

This way you, your friends and family can have more confidence in our overall safety with who we travel with.

We also would like us all to contribute in our own small way to help lighten our Carbon footprint.
